# Milo

Milo is a veterinarian and doctor that works in [[Furry town]({{ site.baseurl }}\Lore\Locations\FurryTown)] and he met [Zlime]({{ site.baseurl }}\Lore\Fursonas\Zlime) when when they were hiding in an alleyway one night and he took them in. He taught Zlime how to read, write and speak. He also helped Zlime get to [Furry town].
