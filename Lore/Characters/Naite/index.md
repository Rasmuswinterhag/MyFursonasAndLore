# Naite

Naite is a scientist in the medical industry that has been working after hours in his job’s laboratory on a personal, unregulated project. [RejuveNaite]({{ site.baseurl }}\Lore\Characters\RejuveNaite) is ideally going to be a miracle cure of sorts, since it would rebuild the body by changing the body to be closer to how your body is supposed to be naturally.
