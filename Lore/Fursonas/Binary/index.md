### Binary, The Protogen
Binary was designed to be a hacker as a troop ready for cyber-war, made to create malware and other programs to help win or get information, but once managed to free their mind from partial stasis and roam free. Now they use their knowledge of computers and coding to make games and be tech support to their friends.
