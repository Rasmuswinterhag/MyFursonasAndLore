# Binary's Escape
Binary was sitting connected to the interface on their captors planet, mindlessly working at creating a virus to attack their enemies systems with. While they were stress testing their code something in their terminal popped and the terminal rebooted. This is not that unusual so it didnt raise any alarms for anyone. However, when it was back on it felt like Binary woke up from a dram. They were in control again. They continued working and acted as nothing was wrong. But instead of working on the last virus, they started on something new. Something to free them and hopefully others like Binary in the future. It was a program that would release them from the terminal but still in the system show that they were there by faking activity like adding or removing text or files from projects. That would mean that they could leave and find a ship and escape.

When Binary had sneaked through the corridors and made their way to the hangars they tried to open ship after ship. most of them locked, until they found a tiny one man stunt ship, this one is rented out to rich people to take for a joyride, it was unlocked. Those are hard to manouver and dont have alot of fuel in them but they can go really fast. As Binary sat down in the seat and started it up people noticed and tried to stop them but luckily they were too late and they made it out. They didnt know where to go now, but they headed into space trying to find somewhere to start anew, turning off the thrusters to conserve fuel and just drifting around in space.

When sleeping in the increasingly uncomfortable seat they suddenly got woken up by an alarm blaring:   
"ON COLLITION COURSE WITH PLANET, STEER AWAY" the robotic voice repeated.   
Looking out the window they saw a massive planet right in they but the thrusters in reverse but didnt have enough power in them to escape the gravitational pull at this point, they managed to slow down enough to go from a deadly crash landing to a survivalable crash landing.
