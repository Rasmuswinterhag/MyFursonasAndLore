# Binary's Escape
[Binary]({{ site.baseurl }}\Lore\Fursonas\Binary) was sitting connected to the interface on their captors planet, mindlessly working at creating a virus to attack their enemies systems with. While they were stress testing their code something in their terminal popped and the terminal rebooted. This is not that unusual so it didnt raise any alarms for anyone. However, when it was back on it felt like Binary woke up from a dram. They were in control again. They continued working and acted as nothing was wrong. But instead of working on the last virus, they started on something new. Something to free them and hopefully others like Binary in the future. It was a program that would release them from the terminal but still in the system show that they were there by faking activity like adding or removing text or files from projects. That would mean that they could leave and find a ship and escape.

When Binary had sneaked through the corridors and made their way to the hangars they tried to open ship after ship. most of them locked, until they found a tiny one man stunt ship, this one is rented out to rich people to take for a joyride, it was unlocked. Those are hard to manouver and dont have alot of fuel in them but they can go really fast. As Binary sat down in the seat and started it up people noticed and tried to stop them but luckily they were too late and they made it out. They didnt know where to go now, but they headed into space trying to find somewhere to start anew, turning off the thrusters to conserve fuel and just drifting around in space.

When sleeping in the increasingly uncomfortable seat they suddenly got woken up by an alarm blaring:   
"ON COLLITION COURSE WITH PLANET, STEER AWAY" the robotic voice repeated.   
Looking out the window they saw a massive planet right in front of them. They put the thrusters in reverse but they didnt have enough power in them to escape the gravitational pull at this point, they managed to slow down enough to go from a deadly crash landing to a survivalable crash landing.

---

[Zlime]({{ site.baseurl }}\Lore\Fursonas\Zlime) and some other people of [[Furry town]({{ site.baseurl }}\Lore\Locations\FurryTown)] looked up at the sky and saw a streak going across the sky. It seems that something is coming in, closer and closer to earth. When it landed it seemed that it wasn't too far from [Furry town], so they sent out a group to check it out, they guessed it was a meteorite.

They found the supprisingly small crater and metal peices thrown around. When they looked in the crater they found what looked like a small ship, but heavily damaged in the crash. They hesitantly approched the wreckage and through the windshield they saw something they didnt recognize, it looked like a person, or a robot? or a cyborg? Whatever it is they are, the people try to break the window open, but to no avail, Space glass is very strong apparently. Suddenly Binary wakes up, first afraid from all the banging, but it quickly stops as they. The people on the outside yelling to eachother they the creature is awake. They hesitantly presses a button that opens the front door.

Binary steps out with their arms raised in front of them in shoulder height ready to defend themselves if attacked. One of the people takes a step forward to try to comunicate   
"We wont hurt you. Who are you, and where did you come from?"   
Binary didnt respond, They had no idea what they were saying, but based of the crowds body language they didnt seem agressive, they seemed more curious. Binary started their built in language learning software that can decrypt spoken language or text given enough of it. They tried to get the people to talk more to hopefully get enough to be able to understand a little bit and maybe say something themselves.

After managing to register down a bit they managed to puzzle together what they hoped the others could understand.  
"Me Escape, Land here, Where here?"  
"Well you're on earth, and we are a group from a town not far from here. You seem like you could use a place to stay. Why not join us?"  
"This earth. I join you to town?"  

Binary followed the group and they talked on their way back. Binary getting better at the language with each sentence.

---

A while later the town had gathered when the group came back, but they weren't alone. They came back with what looked like a robot? or something? The robot-maybe-not-robot introduced themselves as Binary and they said they are a so called protogen, apparently they are part mechanical and part biological and they come from a distant planet that Binary escaped from.
